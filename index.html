About Me

My fascination with technology began in third grade with gadgets from my father, which sparked a deep curiosity that has shaped my academic and professional journey. By tenth grade, I was interested in studying Information Technology, but my school no longer offered it. I then opted to study Computer Application Technology instead. This has lead me to pursue a Bachelor of Computing, specializing in software engineering, at Belgium Campus University after graduating high school. Over the past two years, my knowledge has expanded, especially through networking with other developers, which has introduced me to new techniques and concepts. I've learned that staying updated is crucial in the ever-evolving IT field and that continuous learning is essential for growth.

As I enter my third year and prepare for my dissertation and thesis with a keen interest in data science, I am eager to begin my career in data science, where I can apply my passion for learning, growth, and creativity. I am seeking an internship with a company that values these attributes and offers opportunities for skill development and impactful contributions. Driven by ambition and a commitment to lifelong improvement, I am excited to bring fresh perspectives, a strong work ethic, and a curiosity-driven approach to any team.
